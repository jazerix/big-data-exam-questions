# Hive

## Questions

- How are Hive queries executed in hadoop?
  - They are converted into map-reduce tasks
- What is the purpose of Hive?
  - Provide structure of HDFS files, making SQL queries against them possible 
- What is the metastore?
  - A place where meta data is stored, sometimes a separate database such as MySQL.
- Does Hive work with Avro and Parquet?
  - Yes
